ABOUT US

About Viola

At Viola, we believe in turning dreams into reality. With a passion for creating unforgettable moments and a dedication to exceptional service, we specialize in crafting bespoke weddings that reflect the unique love stories of our clients.

Story

Founded by Wasana Muthumali, Viola was born out of a love for weddings and a desire to make the planning process seamless and enjoyable for couples. Drawing on Sarah's extensive experience in event management and her keen eye for detail, Viola quickly became known for its creativity, professionalism, and personalized approach to wedding planning.

Expertise and Experience

With over a decade of experience in the industry, Viola has planned and executed countless weddings, ranging from intimate ceremonies to extravagant celebrations. Our team of skilled planners brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every event, ensuring that every detail is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed.

Commitment to Excellence

At Viola, excellence is our standard. We approach every wedding with creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail, ensuring that every aspect of the event is executed to perfection. From venue selection to vendor coordination to on-the-day management, we handle every detail with care and precision, allowing our clients to relax and enjoy their special day.

SERVICES

At Viola, we offer a range of comprehensive wedding planning services to ensure that every aspect of your special day is flawless and unforgettable. Whether you're dreaming of an intimate ceremony or a grand celebration, our team is here to bring your vision to life with creativity, expertise, and personalized attention.
